About Whack-a-Mole

Whack-a-Mole is a classic arcade game that tests your reflexes and hand-eye coordination. Moles will randomly pop up from their holes, and your goal is to tap or click on them before they disappear!

The faster you whack, the higher your score. But be careful - the moles get quicker as the game progresses!

How to Play:

  1. Click "Start Game" to begin
  2. Tap or click on moles as they appear
  3. Each successful whack earns you points
  4. Try to get the highest score before time runs out
